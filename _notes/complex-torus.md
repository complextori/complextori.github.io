title: What is a Complex Torus?
---

A **complex torus** of dimension $g$ is a quotient $V / \Lambda$ where $V$ is a finite-dimensional vector space and $\Lambda$ is a lattice in $V$, i.e. a free abelian group with the natural map $\Lambda \otimes \mathbb{R} \to V$an isomorphism.
